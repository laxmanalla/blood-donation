How To Donate

1. Donor must fill the Registration form.
2. Medical Check up (5 mins) Donor Medical History & Life Style is asked, Check up of temperature ,Blood donation, Pulse and Haemoglobin
3. Donation (8 mins) Phlebotomist draws unit(350ml/450ml) of Blood
4. Refreshment(10 mins)
